About


JuneJams is a 2-day codecamp, organised by the National University of Singapore's Women in Tech, for female secondary school students. Students will be able to indulge in programming workshops, fireside chats with current NUS Computing students, sharing sessions with external organisations and inspiring talks from accomplished women in the STEM field for free!
